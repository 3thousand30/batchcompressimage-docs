# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 2026**

BatchCompress Image is developed by [3thousand30](https://3thousand30.com).

---

## What we collect

3thousand30 does not collect, store, or receive your source images, compressed images, filenames, folders, or chosen compression settings through this app.

- No analytics sent to us
- No telemetry sent to us
- No crash reporting sent to us
- No account or registration required

---

## What happens on your device

BatchCompress Image processes your images locally on your Windows device and writes the results to folders on your machine.

Your source images are not uploaded to 3thousand30 servers for compression.

---

## Metadata and exported files

Depending on your settings, the app can either preserve image metadata or remove it.

- If **Strip EXIF metadata** is off, exported files keep embedded metadata where the output format supports it
- If **Strip EXIF metadata** is on, metadata is removed from exported files

Processed files are written to the output folder you choose.
